I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Economics and Business at Universitat Pompeu Fabra, affiliated with the Barcelona School of Economics. I graduated with a PhD in Economics from the University of Zurich in 2023 and I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Economics at Harvard University during the 2023-2024 academic year.

I use theory and experiments to study topics at the intersection of economics and psychology. My research aims to expand our understanding of how behavior is driven by narratives, emotions, and strategic thinking, with a particular focus on the role of beliefs.
